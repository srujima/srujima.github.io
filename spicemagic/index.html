ABOUT

Spice Magic is a pure vegetarian restaurant. Spice Magic invites you to taste our exotic flavours of authentic Indo chinese cuisine with a few delicacies from the north and south regions. At Spice Magic, you will definitely enjoy each dish we prepare. Each dish is individually prepared to your preference to ensure freshness and quality.
